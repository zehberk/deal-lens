How ratings work

  • Price is compared with local KBB Fair Purchase Price, then national FPP or Fair Market Value when needed.
  • A complete rating requires a vehicle-history report. Title, accident, odometer, mileage, service, and warranty evidence can change the risk score and downgrade the deal.
  • Listings without a history report, price, or compatible KBB data are summarized by reason rather than displayed individually.
  • CARFAX warranty status is an estimate and should be confirmed with the manufacturer or dealer.
